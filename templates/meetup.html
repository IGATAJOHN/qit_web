Guest Speaker: Precious Adaeze

Precious Adaeze is a skilled cybersecurity engineer with a strong expertise in penetration testing, SOC (Security Operations Center) analysis, cybersecurity engineering, and ethical hacking. She is a graduate of the Federal University of Technology Minna, located in Niger State, Nigeria. Throughout her career, Precious has demonstrated proficiency in identifying and mitigating security vulnerabilities, ensuring robust defense mechanisms for organizations, and conducting thorough security assessments. Her role as a SOC analyst involves monitoring, detecting, and responding to security incidents, thereby protecting critical information assets from potential threats. In the field of penetration testing, Precious excels at simulating cyberattacks to uncover weaknesses in systems and networks, providing actionable insights to fortify defenses. Her ethical hacking skills are pivotal in helping organizations preemptively address security issues and enhance their overall security posture. Precious Adaeze's comprehensive background and technical acumen make her an invaluable asset in the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity.
